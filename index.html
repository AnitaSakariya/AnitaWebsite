ABOUT ME

Anita Sakariya is born on 17th February 1978 in Takalgarh a village in Rajasthan,India. She did her education in Chennai.Her parents were her real heroes for making her who she is. Her father always thought her many skills and motivated her to be bold and face all challenges in life.Her mother is a spirtual being and follower of Jainisim because of which she is also a true path follower of loed Mahavira. Later she enrolled her children in a religious forum called Sanskar Vatika. She felt that she had a lot to give and learn so she enrolled herself as a teacher. In this journey she got many oppurtunities to grow and learn from that time there was no turning back. In her parenting journey she discovered herself as a teacher, script writer, director, poet, orator and a author.
